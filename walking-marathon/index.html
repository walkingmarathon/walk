This walk is suitable for all, dogs and older children will be able to complete the full route and even younger children can join in for part of the walk.

In the case of very bad weather, the walk might be cancelled.

Future training walks: 1 March | 29 March | 26 April

The walking marathon relay

Why relay? Because it means walkers of various abilities can participate.

You can choose to walk the whole marathon along the Saxon Shore Way or only a part of it:

Faversham – Seasalter (6.8 miles)
Seasalter – Whitstable (1.4 miles)
Whitstable – Herne Bay (5 miles)
Herne Bay – Birchington (8.7 miles)
Birchington – Margate (3.8 miles)

Walking with children?

Choose a route depending on their age and abilities. You could also decide to walk for only a bit of the marathon and then enjoy a day at the beach. There are several great beaches to choose from: Whitstable, Herne Bay, Minnis Bay or Margate.
